About Us

PUSAK is a voluntary, non-political organization formed by students studying in various public universities across Bangladesh who originate from the naturally beautiful Kamalganj Upazila of Moulvibazar.

Inspired by the deep greenery of Lawachara Rainforest, the endless tea gardens, the serene Hakaluki Haor, and the cultural harmony of the region, PUSAK works to support education, social welfare, youth development, relief programs, and community progress.
